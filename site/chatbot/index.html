    Chatbot questions

    A Chatbot is another way to answer questions. Chatbots require development so a limited number of questions may be implemented. Common questions are usually implemented first.

    Chatbot questions may be:

    • about the collection of factsheets, e.g. "How many applications do we have?"
    • about a specific factsheet name, e.g. "Who is responsible for the application Dynamics?".
    • small talk to test the Chatbot's capabilities, e.g. "Are you a robot?” or "What can you do?"

    The Chatbot will respond with:

    • the answer, e.g. "Alex Bailey is responsible for the application Dynamics 365"
    • a clarifying question, e.g. "Do you mean Dynamics 365 Marketing or Dynamics 365 Field Service?"
    • how to access the answer if it can't be displayed, e.g. "To see a list of all applications and who is responsible, view the Inventory."
    • an apology for not answering the question, and a call to action e.g. "I'm sorry, I can only answer these questions."
    • small talk and a call to action, e.g. "I'm a very clever robot. Ask me something."

    1. Which applications do user group Customers use?

    Business Capabilities

    1. Which applications support business capability Customer Management?
    2. What is the IT cost of business capability Human Resource Management?

    Processes

    1. Who is responsible for process Onboard Staff?
    2. Which applications support process Define Project Portfolio?
      1. Which business critical applications support process Identify Customer?

    Applications

    1. How many applications do we have?
    2. Show me application Dynamics 365?
    3. Which applications will become end-of-life this year?
    4. Which applications will become active this year?
    5. Who is responsible for an application Dynamics 365?
    6. Who uses application Zoom?
    7. What will replace application Zoom?
    8. What applications have changed to #contain?1
    9. What applications are impacted by project Cloud Migration?
    10. Does application Dynamics 365 process sensitive data?
    11. What is the IT cost of operating application Dynamics 365?

    Data

    1. Who is the Data Steward2 for the data object Customer?
    2. What is the data classification?
    3. How are data objects grouped?
    4. What applications manage or access data?
      1. What applications manage or access sensitive data?
    5. Where is data stored?
      1. Is any sensitive data stored in other countries? i.e. Is there a sovereignty risk?

    Projects

    1. What projects do we have?
      1. How are projects grouped? e.g. Portfolios, Programs, and Projects
    2. When will a project start or end?
    3. What applications will be changed by a project? - (Free Draw diagram? Use smart explorer for a single project)
    4. What user groups are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    5. What business capabilities are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    6. What processes are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    7. What is a project's budget? - (factsheet?)
    8. Is the project budget on track?
    9. Which projects provide the highest value at the lowest risk?

    IT Components

    1. What IT Components do we have?, what are their properties, and who manages them?
    2. Where are IT Components located?
    3. What technology implements an application? IT Component matrix with Application children or data flow or free draw
    4. How is the technology portfolio planned to evolve year by year?
      1. What is the technology roadmap?
      2. What projects are implementing the technology roadmap?
    5. What are our technical standards (preferred product to perform a function)? IT Component landscape with resource classification view - not answering functional bit. Need resource classification between application and business capability
    6. Which are standard technologies and can we reuse a solution?
    7. What is the our technical standards (preferred product to perform a function)? IT Component landscape with resource classification view - not answering functional bit. Need resource classification between application and business capability
    8. Is there opportunity to consolidate technologies?
    9. How is an application hosted and how is this planned to evolve?
    10. What is the planned lifecycle of an IT component

    2. How are technology categories grouped?
    3. What IT components are preferred to implement a technology category?

    Small talk

    People like to test the limits of a chatbot. It's good to respond to smalltalk.

    Greetings

    1. How are you? / How are you doing/going?
    2. What’s up?
    3. Good morning/evening/afternoon/night
    4. Tell me something
    5. Ok / Yes / I’ll do that now
    6. Hello, Thank you, Goodbye
    7. How can you help me? / what can you do?
    8. Hi, my name is……
    9. Happy birthday!
    10. I have a question / can you help me?

    Fun phrases

    1. Do you know a joke? / You’re funny!
    2. Do you love me? / I love you
    3. Will you marry me? / Are you single?
    4. Do you like people?
    5. Does Santa Claus exist?
    6. Are you part of the Matrix?
    7. You’re cute / beautiful / handsome
    8. Do you have a hobby?
    9. You’re smart / clever / intelligent
    10. Tell me about your personality

    Phrases from users that are NOT HAPPY

    1. !#$#% (swear words)
    2. You’re annoying / you suck / you’re boring/bad /crazy
    3. I want to speak to a human / live agent / customer service
    4. Don’t you speak English?!
    5. I want the answer NOW!

    Testing questions

    1. Are you human? / Are you a robot?
    2. What is your name?
    3. How old are you? / What’s your age?
    4. What day is it today?
    5. What do you do with my data? / Do you save what I say?
    6. Who made you?
    7. Which languages can you speak?
    8. What is your mother’s name?
    9. Where do you live?
    10. What’s the weather like today?
    11. Are you expensive?
    12. Who’s your boss / master?
    13. Do you get smarter?
