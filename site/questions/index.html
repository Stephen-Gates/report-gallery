    Questions you can answer with LeanIX reports

    • The questions below are arranged by the factsheets involved in answering the question.
    • Questions answered by using a built-in view are shown as sub-questions.
    • Questions based on a view using tags are marked with [tags]. Tags are unique to your LeanIX implementation.
    • Where I don't know how best to phrase a question, there may be an alternative phrasing as a sub-question, marked with [alternative].
      • My leaning is to put the factsheet that provides the answer as the first part of the question but sometime this doesn't sound natural.
      • If questions are phrased how people commonly ask, then it will be easier to turn this resource into a chatbot.

    User Groups

    1. What user groups are part of, or interact with, our organisation?
      1. Which user groups are #internal to our organisation? [tags]
      2. Which user groups are #external to our organisation? [tags]
      3. Which #external user groups use our #internet channel? [tags]
    2. How are user groups structured?
    3. What applications support user groups?
      1. Which applications do #external user groups use? [tags]
    4. Where are application users located?

    Business Capabilities

    1. What business capabilities support our organisation?
    2. How are business capabilities structured?
    3. Which applications support business capabilities?
      1. Which business capabilities are supported by applications? [alternative]
      2. Which business capabilities are impacted when an application fails?
    4. What is the IT cost of supporting business capabilities?

    Processes

    1. What processes does our organisation perform?
      1. Who are the stakeholders?
    2. How are processes structured?
    3. Which applications support processes?
      1. What processes are impacted when an application fails?
      2. Which applications are business critical in supporting a process?

    Applications

    1. What applications do we have?
      1. Who are the stakeholders?
      2. Who is responsible for an application?
    2. How many applications do we have?
    3. When are applications planned to change?
      1. When will successor applications replace existing applications?
    4. What applications are used by a user group to support a business capability?
    5. What applications are used by a user group to perform a process?
      1. Who is impacted when an application fails? - (smart explorer)
      2. Which applications are business critical for a user group?
    6. Where are applications used?
    7. Which applications support business capabilities?
      1. Can applications supporting the same business capability be rationalised?
    8. For a group of related functions, what is the overall driver for change, intent and roadmap?
    9. For applications implementing a group of related functions, what is the target application, roadmap, and what is the status of the roadmap plan?
    10. What applications are critical to support which business capabilities?
    11. What applications are at risk due to obsolescent underlying IT components?
    12. What is the health of applications and what action is required? (TIME: Tolerate, Invest, Migrate, Enhance) based on functional and technical fitness survey and other inputs.
    13. In which applications should we invest, which divest?
    14. Where are applications hosted?
    15. What applications are impacted by projects? - free draw, project roadmap with applications as children
    16. Which projects impact applications? [alternative]
    17. Does an application process any sensitive data, how is this data classified, where is it located?
    18. What is the IT cost of operating applications?
    19. What applications are used to support a business capability used to perform a process?
    20. For a group of related functions, what applications implement these, what is there status and roadmap?
    21. How does the application portfolio change over time (planned and proposed)?
    22. What changes have occurred in the application portfolio since the last reporting period?
      1. applications added, retired, change in status, renamed, change in roadmap dates, change in intent, etc.
      2. resource classification change
      3. lifecycle date changes (new Active, new Phase-out, End-of-life)
    23. How many applications are active or being phased out each month?
    24. How many applications became active each year?

    Data

    1. What data objects do we use?
      1. Who are the stakeholders?
      2. What is the data classification?
    2. How are data objects structured?
    3. What applications manage or access data?
      1. What applications manage or access sensitive data?
    4. Where is data stored?
      1. Is any sensitive data stored in other countries? i.e. Is there a sovereignty risk?

    Interfaces

    1. What interfaces do we use?
    2. Which applications provide or consume interfaces?

    Projects

    1. What projects do we have?
    2. When will a project start or end?
    3. What user groups are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    4. What business capabilities are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    5. What processes are impacted by projects because of the applications they change?
    6. Is the project budget on track?
    7. Which projects provide the highest value at the lowest risk?

    IT Components

    1. What IT Components do we have?, what are their properties, and who manages them?
    2. Where are IT Components located?
    3. What technology implements an application?
    4. How is the technology portfolio planned to evolve year by year?
      1. What is the technology roadmap?
      2. What projects are implementing the technology roadmap?
    5. What are our technical standards (preferred product to perform a function)? IT Component landscape with resource classification view - not answering functional bit. Need resource classification between application and business capability
    6. Which are standard technologies and can we reuse a solution?
    7. What is the our technical standards (preferred product to perform a function)? IT Component landscape with resource classification view - not answering functional bit. Need resource classification between application and business capability
    8. Is there opportunity to consolidate technologies?
    9. How is an application hosted and how is this planned to evolve?
    10. What is the planned lifecycle of an IT component

    Technical Stack

    1. What technology categories do we use to classify IT components?
    2. How are technology categories structured?
    3. What IT components are preferred to implement a technology category?

    Providers

    1. What providers do we use?
    2. What IT components does a provider supply?
    3. What is the risk of a provider?
    4. Is there an opportunity to reduce the number of providers we deal with by changing technologies
    5. What is the total IT spend by provider?
    6. Which provider relationships need closer management?
