    Choose a report to discover the questions it can answer and how to re-create it using LeanIX.

    Factsheet Map reports show a hierarchy of one factsheet type.

    Landscape reports show one factsheet type within the hierarchy of another.

    Matrix reports show one factsheet type within a matrix of other factsheet types or time.

    Roadmap reports show the lifecycle of factsheets over time.

    The Interface Circle Map shows a high-level view of interfaces between applications in the portfolio.

    Data Flow diagrams show interfaces between selected applications.

    Location reports show a geographic map of user group or IT component locations.

    Cost reports total to costs associated with a business capability, project or provider.

    Portfolio reports show a grid of applications, projects or providers measured against two criteria.

    Lifecycle and Age reports show the number of applications added or removed from the portfolio over time.

    The Inventory shows a table of factsheets and their properties.
